OPERA AIDA BRANDING

UNIVERSITY PROJECT

I was asked to design a poster, book cover, and tickets for an Opera Aida event using a set of given information as part of a university typography course. Opera Aida is a beutiful piece of Italian opera that tells the story of an Egyptian love affair, and I chose to highlight this combination in an experimental design by intertwining a neutral typeface with classic Arabic calligraphy.

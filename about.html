Who am I?

I’m a little quirky and I love learning and trying new things -- both qualities that show up very clearly in all my work. With my love and passion for graphic design, and an added touch of spice from my background in computer science, I’m always trying to create things that are functional and practical, but do so in the most elegant and beautiful ways.
